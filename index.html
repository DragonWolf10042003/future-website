Laser engraving is truly an amazing art and great way to create different things. Through this site you will be able to choose wood that you want, and be able to upload what you want on it. The design can include images and text or just one of them. Does not need both. Please select if you want it just as a decoration, coaster or key fob which will be in the setup process.

Then the request will be sent in, and I will arrange the uploaded content accordingly to make it look nice. The sizes of the pieces of wood will be 4x4 inches, so do please keep that in mind while you are adding what you want laser engraved. The key fob will be 1.5x3 inches so it can go on a keychain. Larger images will be sized down to fit. Go to the wood selection page to get started!
